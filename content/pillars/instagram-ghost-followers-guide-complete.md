## Understanding Ghost Followers

Ghost followers are accounts that appear in a follower list but rarely engage. Low activity alone does not prove that an account is fake, so use several signals and avoid removing people solely from one automated score.

## Review Before Taking Action

Use exported follower data to understand relationships, then review questionable accounts manually in Instagram. SafeUnfollow does not automate removals or require direct account access.
