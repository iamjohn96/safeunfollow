## What Are Ghost Followers?

Ghost followers are inactive Instagram accounts that follow you but never engage — no likes, no comments, no story views. They inflate your follower count while tanking your engagement rate.

## Why Ghost Followers Hurt Your Reach

Instagram's algorithm measures engagement rate: likes + comments divided by followers. If you have 5,000 followers but only 50 likes per post, your rate is 1% — the algorithm treats your content as low quality and reduces its reach.

## FAQ

**How many ghost followers is normal?**
For most accounts, 10–20% is typical. Focus on engagement rate improvement, not vanity metrics.

**Will removing ghost followers increase my reach?**
Yes. Fewer inactive followers means your engagement rate improves, which signals quality to the algorithm.
