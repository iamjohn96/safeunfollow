## The Evolution of Instagram Strategies

In the early days of Instagram, the follow/unfollow method was a common, albeit often frowned upon, tactic for rapid growth. The idea was simple: follow many accounts, hoping some would follow back, then unfollow those who didn't. This strategy aimed to boost follower counts quickly.

However, Instagram has continuously refined its algorithms to prioritize genuine interactions and penalize manipulative tactics. Accounts engaging in aggressive follow/unfollow patterns are now often flagged for suspicious activity, leading to temporary action blocks or reduced visibility. This shift makes it clear that relying on such methods for sustainable growth is no longer viable.
