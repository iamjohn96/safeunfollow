## What is the Instagram Unfollow Limit?
The Instagram unfollow limit is the maximum number of accounts you can unfollow within a certain time frame. This limit is in place to prevent users from mass unfollowing, which can be seen as a spamming behavior by Instagram's algorithms. The exact number of the limit is not publicly disclosed by Instagram, but it is generally believed to be around 50 to 100 unfollows per hour for accounts with fewer followers, and slightly higher for accounts with more followers. However, these numbers can vary based on the account's history, engagement, and other factors.
