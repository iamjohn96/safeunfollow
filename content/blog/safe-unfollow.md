Instagram's API is not designed for third-party apps to track unfollowers in this manner, making such methods inherently risky.

Furthermore, these apps often store your login information on their servers, creating a potential data breach point. Your privacy is compromised, and you have little control over how your data is used or protected. For anyone serious about maintaining their Instagram account's integrity and their personal data's security, these traditional methods are simply not a viable or safe option.
